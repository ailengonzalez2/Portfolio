Traviu

Design UX/UI

Technologies: Figma

Traviu is an application designed to assist users in managing their travel budgets effectively. Users can track their expenses and monitor their spending through a user-friendly dashboard, ensuring they stay within the predefined budget limits for each trip. In case they approach or exceed the budget limits, the app provides timely alerts, empowering users to regulate their spending and maintain financial stability throughout their travels.
